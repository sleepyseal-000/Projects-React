# Web Development Project 1 - *Community Board*

Submitted by: **Miranda Casan**

This web app: **The NYC Food Hub is a community-led initiative focused on promoting access to healthy, affordable, and sustainable food options for all New Yorkers. The project is driven by a group of passionate community members who believe that everyone in the city should have access to fresh, nutritious food, regardless of their income or location.**
